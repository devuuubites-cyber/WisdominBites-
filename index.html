The Antidote

Upgrade Your Mental Hardware

To navigate a complex world, you don't need to read every book or article. You need Mental Models.

Mental models are the foundational frameworks used by the world's greatest thinkers—from philosophers to billionaire investors. They are the "apps" you install in your brain to process reality more accurately.

WisdomInBites distills these massive concepts into clear, digestible, 2-minute readings that you can apply immediately to your life and career.

First Principles Thinking
Boil things down to their fundamental truths and reason up from there, avoiding analogy.
Inversion
Approach a problem backward. Instead of asking how to succeed, ask how you could guarantee failure.
Second-Order Effects
Don't just look at the immediate result of an action. Look at the consequences of the consequences.
